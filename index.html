Cooking Steps

  • In a heavy 2-quart saucepan, mix cocoa powder and sugar.
  • Over medium-high heat, gradually whisk milk and 1/2 cup of the whipping cream. Simmer gently 25 to 35 minutes.
  • Meanwhile, whip remaining 1/2 cup whipping cream and the sour cream to form soft peaks; refrigerate.
  • Remove cocoa mixture from heat and add the chopped chocolate; stir until chocolate is completely melted.
  • Spoon 2 tablespoons hot cocoa mixture into each of the four 8-ounce.
  • Top each with a scoop of ice cream and 2 tablespoons of whipped cream; repeat layer once, ending with whipped cream.

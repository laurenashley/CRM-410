Cooking Steps

  • Introduction
  • Over medium-high heat, gradually whisk milk and 1/2 cup of the whipping cream. Simmer gently 25 to 35 minutes.
  • Mix Milkmaid and vanilla essence together.
  • Fold in the cream.
  • Pour into mold and cover with foil. Freeze for 2 hours.
  • Whip when half set and freeze for a few more hours.
  • Scoop and serve.
